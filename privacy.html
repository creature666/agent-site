Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

Data Collection

This application accesses Google Workspace data (Google Sheets, Google Docs, Google Drive) exclusively on behalf of the sole authorized user. The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

Data Usage

All data access is performed in real-time at the user's explicit request. The application reads and writes data only within the user's own Google Workspace account. No data is cached, logged, or stored outside the user's Google ecosystem.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third parties. The application has no analytics, tracking, or advertising components.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the repository owner.
